# Racecadotril + Saccharomyces boulardii + Lactobacillus

**Generic Name:** Racecadotril + *Saccharomyces boulardii* + *Lactobacillus*

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to treat acute diarrhea, particularly in cases where the cause is suspected to be infectious.  It combines the actions of three different components to offer a multifaceted approach to managing diarrhea symptoms.  Racecadotril reduces the severity and frequency of bowel movements by modulating the gut's response to the inflammatory process underlying diarrhea.  *Saccharomyces boulardii*, a probiotic yeast, helps restore the balance of gut microbiota, which can be disrupted during diarrhea.  *Lactobacillus*, another probiotic, further supports this gut microbiota restoration and contributes to maintaining a healthy intestinal environment.  While primarily used for acute diarrhea, this combination may sometimes be considered for managing symptoms of traveler's diarrhea or diarrhea associated with antibiotic use, though its use in these cases should be determined by a healthcare professional.  It's crucial to note that this combination is not a treatment for all types of diarrhea, and a proper diagnosis is essential.


**Dosage:**  Dosage varies considerably based on the specific formulation (e.g., tablet, suspension, capsule), age, weight, and severity of the diarrhea.  **Always follow the instructions provided by your healthcare provider or those found on the medication's label.**  There is no universally standardized dosage across all products containing this combination, as the ratio of each component may vary.  Adult and child dosages are typically adjusted based on the manufacturer's recommendations.  This information is not a substitute for professional medical advice; always consult a physician or pharmacist before starting any medication regimen.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:** Nausea, abdominal bloating or discomfort, flatulence.
* **Less Common but Important:** Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling), headache.
* **Serious (Rare):** Severe allergic reactions (anaphylaxis).  Seek immediate medical attention if you experience symptoms such as difficulty breathing, swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat.

It's essential to note that not everyone experiences side effects, and the severity can vary significantly. If you experience any side effects, particularly those that are persistent or concerning, contact your doctor or pharmacist.


**How it Works:**

This combination medication works through a synergistic mechanism:

* **Racecadotril:** This drug acts by inhibiting enkephalinase, an enzyme that breaks down enkephalins, natural pain-relieving and anti-diarrheal substances in the gut.  By inhibiting this enzyme, racecadotril allows these natural substances to work more effectively, reducing bowel motility and the frequency of bowel movements.
* ***Saccharomyces boulardii*:** This probiotic yeast helps to restore the normal balance of the gut flora, which is often disrupted during diarrhea.  It competes with harmful bacteria, reducing their ability to cause further inflammation and damage.
* ***Lactobacillus*:**  Similar to *S. boulardii*, *Lactobacillus* is a probiotic bacterium that aids in restoring the healthy gut microbiota.  It produces beneficial substances, further supporting gut health and reducing diarrhea symptoms.

These three components work together to lessen the severity and duration of diarrhea, promoting quicker recovery.


**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:**  This combination should not be used in individuals with known allergies to any of its components.
* **Drug Interactions:**  Potentially significant interactions can occur with other medications, especially those affecting gut motility or metabolism.  Always inform your doctor or pharmacist about all other medications, supplements, and herbal remedies you are taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:**  The safety of this combination during pregnancy and breastfeeding hasn't been fully established.  Discuss the risks and benefits with your doctor before using this medication if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.
* **Children and Older Adults:**  Dosage adjustments may be necessary for children and older adults due to differences in metabolism and potential underlying health conditions.  A healthcare professional should determine the appropriate dose.
