# Methylcobalamin + Alpha Lipoic Acid + Myo-Inositol + Folic Acid + Chromium Picolinate + Selenium + Benfotiamine

**Generic Name:** Methylcobalamin + Alpha Lipoic Acid + Myo-Inositol + Folic Acid + Chromium Picolinate + Selenium + Benfotiamine

**Usage:** This combination of nutrients is primarily used to support nerve health and metabolic function.  It's often employed to address symptoms associated with peripheral neuropathy (nerve damage in the arms and legs), often seen in conditions like diabetes.  The individual components each play a role:

* **Methylcobalamin (Vitamin B12):** Crucial for nerve function and the production of red blood cells.  A deficiency can lead to neurological problems.
* **Alpha-Lipoic Acid:** A powerful antioxidant that helps protect cells from damage and improves glucose metabolism, beneficial for those with diabetes.
* **Myo-Inositol:**  Plays a role in various cellular processes, including insulin signaling and nerve function. It's sometimes used to address insulin resistance and polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS).
* **Folic Acid (Vitamin B9):** Essential for cell growth and development, particularly important during pregnancy and for red blood cell production.
* **Chromium Picolinate:** Helps improve insulin sensitivity, potentially aiding in blood sugar control.
* **Selenium:** An antioxidant that protects cells from damage.
* **Benfotiamine:** A fat-soluble form of vitamin B1, thought to improve glucose metabolism and nerve function, particularly beneficial in diabetic neuropathy.


This combination isn't a treatment for a specific disease but rather a nutritional supplement aimed at supporting overall health and mitigating symptoms associated with several conditions, mainly those impacting nerve function and metabolism. It's important to note that it's not a replacement for prescribed medication.


**Dosage:**  Dosage varies significantly depending on the specific product and the individual's needs.  There is no universally standardized dosage for this combination.  Always follow the instructions on the product label or as directed by a healthcare professional.  The dosage will consider factors like age, health status, and the specific condition being addressed.  Generally, it's available in tablet form.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:** Mild gastrointestinal upset (nausea, diarrhea, constipation), headache, skin rash.
* **Less Common but Serious:** Allergic reactions (anaphylaxis), liver problems (rare but requires immediate medical attention).


**Note:**  If you experience any adverse effects, discontinue use and consult a doctor immediately.


**How it Works:** Each component works independently and synergistically.  The combination aims to support nerve health by providing essential nutrients for nerve function (B vitamins, myo-inositol), protecting nerves from damage (antioxidants like alpha-lipoic acid and selenium), and improving glucose metabolism (alpha-lipoic acid, chromium, benfotiamine, myo-inositol) to prevent or mitigate nerve damage caused by high blood sugar.
