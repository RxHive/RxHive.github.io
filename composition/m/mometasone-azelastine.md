# Mometasone + Azelastine

**Generic Name:** Mometasone + Azelastine

**Usage:**

Mometasone + azelastine nasal spray is primarily used to treat the symptoms of seasonal allergic rhinitis (hay fever) in adults and children aged 12 years and older.  This combination medication targets the two main components of allergic reactions: inflammation and itching.  Mometasone, a corticosteroid, reduces inflammation in the nasal passages, while azelastine, an antihistamine, blocks the action of histamine, a chemical released during allergic reactions that causes itching, sneezing, and runny nose. While its primary use is for seasonal allergies, it may also provide some relief from symptoms of perennial allergic rhinitis (allergies present year-round), although this isn't always explicitly stated in prescribing information.  Always consult a doctor to determine if this medication is appropriate for your specific allergy symptoms and type.

**Dosage:**

The typical dosage for adults and children 12 years and older is one spray in each nostril once daily.  Dosage adjustments based on age, weight, or disease severity are not generally recommended;  the prescribed dose should be followed unless otherwise instructed by a physician.  This medication is only available as a nasal spray.  Before the first use, the spray should be primed according to the instructions on the product packaging.  Proper technique is crucial for effective delivery of the medication to the nasal passages.  It is important to gently blow your nose before administering the spray.


**Side Effects:**

Common side effects may include:

* Nosebleeds
* Headache
* Burning or stinging sensation in the nose
* Nasal dryness
* Cough
* Bad taste in the mouth


Less common, but potentially serious, side effects may include:

* Allergic reactions (rash, hives, swelling)
* Increased risk of infection in the nose
* Glaucoma (increased pressure in the eye)
* Cataracts
* Impaired wound healing


If you experience any of these side effects, especially serious ones, discontinue use and seek medical attention immediately.


**How it Works:**

Mometasone and azelastine work synergistically to alleviate allergic rhinitis symptoms. Mometasone, a corticosteroid, reduces inflammation by suppressing the release of inflammatory mediators in the nasal passages. This leads to a decrease in swelling, congestion, and nasal discharge. Azelastine, an antihistamine, blocks the action of histamine, a key chemical responsible for the itching, sneezing, watery eyes, and runny nose associated with allergies. By combining these two mechanisms of action, the medication effectively targets both the inflammatory and allergic components of the condition, providing broader relief.


**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** This medication should not be used by individuals with known hypersensitivity to either mometasone furoate or azelastine hydrochloride or any of the inactive ingredients.
* **Drug Interactions:** While interactions are generally rare, it's essential to inform your doctor about all medications, including over-the-counter drugs, herbal supplements, and vitamins, that you are currently taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:**  Consult your doctor before using this medication if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.
* **Children:** This medication is typically not recommended for children under 12 years of age.
* **Elderly:**  While generally well-tolerated in the elderly, caution should be exercised, and close monitoring may be needed due to potential increased sensitivity to side effects.
* **Other Conditions:**  Individuals with glaucoma, cataracts, or impaired wound healing should use caution and inform their doctor before use.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: How long does it take to work?** A:  You may notice some symptom relief within a few days, but full therapeutic benefit may take several weeks.
* **Q: Can I use this medication with other nasal sprays?** A:  It's best to talk to your doctor before combining this with other nasal medications to avoid potential interactions or reduced effectiveness.
* **Q: What should I do if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it's almost time for your next dose.  Do not double the dose.
* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store at room temperature, away from direct sunlight and moisture.  Keep out of reach of children.
* **Q: Is this medication habit-forming?** A: No, this medication is not habit-forming.
