# Methylcobalamin + Alpha Lipoic Acid + Folic Acid + Inositol + Chromium polynicotinate + Selenomethionine + Zinc monomethionine

**Generic Name:** Methylcobalamin + Alpha Lipoic Acid + Folic Acid + Inositol + Chromium Polynicotinate + Selenomethionine + Zinc Monomethionine

**Usage:** This combination of nutrients is primarily used to support nerve health and overall metabolic function.  It's often recommended for individuals experiencing symptoms associated with nerve damage (neuropathy), particularly peripheral neuropathy, which can manifest as tingling, numbness, pain, or weakness in the extremities.  It may also be used to address deficiencies in the individual components.

This combination targets several metabolic pathways.  For instance, methylcobalamin (a form of vitamin B12) and folic acid are crucial for nerve function and DNA synthesis. Alpha-lipoic acid acts as an antioxidant, protecting cells from damage. Inositol plays a role in various cellular processes, including nerve signal transmission. Chromium polynicotinate helps improve insulin sensitivity. Selenomethionine and zinc monomethionine are important trace minerals with antioxidant and enzymatic roles, contributing to overall health.  While this combination supports various functions, it's not a cure for any specific disease but rather a supportive therapy aimed at improving symptoms and overall well-being.  Specific uses may vary based on the practitioner's assessment of individual needs.

**How it Works:** Each component in this combination plays a specific role:

* **Methylcobalamin:**  A crucial B vitamin, essential for nerve function and the formation of myelin (the protective sheath around nerves).
* **Alpha-Lipoic Acid:**  A potent antioxidant, reducing oxidative stress and protecting nerve cells from damage.
* **Folic Acid:**  Essential for DNA synthesis and cell growth, crucial for nerve health.
* **Inositol:**  Involved in numerous cellular processes, including signal transmission in nerves.
* **Chromium Polynicotinate:**  Enhances insulin sensitivity, improving glucose metabolism.
* **Selenomethionine:**  An antioxidant trace mineral, protecting cells from oxidative damage.
* **Zinc Monomethionine:**  An essential trace mineral with various enzymatic roles and antioxidant properties.

The synergistic action of these components is believed to provide comprehensive support for nerve health and metabolic function.
