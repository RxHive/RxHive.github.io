# Aluminium Hydroxide + Dimethicone + Milk Of Magnesia

**Generic Name:** Aluminium Hydroxide + Dimethicone + Milk of Magnesia

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to relieve the symptoms of heartburn, acid indigestion, and upset stomach.  It's a triple-action formula addressing different aspects of these conditions.  Aluminium hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide (the active component of Milk of Magnesia) are antacids, neutralizing stomach acid. Dimethicone is an anti-foaming agent that helps relieve gas and bloating, often associated with these digestive issues.  While primarily used for symptomatic relief, it can indirectly help manage conditions like peptic ulcers by reducing stomach acid's irritating effects.  However, it's crucial to remember this combination doesn't treat the underlying cause of these conditions; it only manages symptoms.  For persistent or severe digestive problems, consulting a doctor for proper diagnosis and treatment is essential.

**Dosage:**  Dosage varies depending on the specific formulation (tablet, liquid suspension, etc.) and the manufacturer.  Always follow the instructions on the product label carefully.  Generally, adults take one to two tablets or the recommended dose of liquid suspension as needed, usually after meals or at bedtime.  Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.  Children's dosages vary significantly and should only be determined by a healthcare professional based on age and weight. This medication is not typically given to infants or very young children without explicit medical guidance.

**Side Effects:**

* **Common:** Constipation (more likely with aluminum hydroxide), diarrhea (more likely with magnesium hydroxide), gas. These are generally mild and temporary.
* **Less Common but Serious:**  Severe constipation or diarrhea, dehydration (especially if diarrhea is prolonged),  allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling).
* **Rare:**  Kidney problems (primarily with prolonged high-dose use).

If you experience any of these side effects, particularly the serious ones, or if any side effect persists or worsens, stop using the medication and contact your doctor or pharmacist immediately.


**How it Works:**

This medication works through a three-pronged approach:

* **Antacids:** Aluminium hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide neutralize stomach acid by chemically reacting with it, thus reducing acidity and relieving heartburn and indigestion.
* **Anti-foaming Agent:** Dimethicone helps to break up gas bubbles in the stomach and intestines, reducing bloating and discomfort.  This makes it easier for gas to be expelled.

The combined action of these three components provides comprehensive relief from the symptoms of indigestion and heartburn.

**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** This medication is not suitable for individuals with known allergies to any of its components.  Individuals with kidney disease should exercise extreme caution and consult their doctor before use as prolonged use may cause problems.
* **Drug Interactions:**  This medication can interact with other medications, particularly those that require a specific stomach pH for absorption.  Inform your doctor or pharmacist about all the medications, vitamins, and supplements you are taking.
* **Specific Populations:** Pregnant or breastfeeding women, and older adults should consult their doctor before using this medication.  Children's use requires specific medical guidance.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: How long can I take this medication?** A:  For short-term relief of occasional heartburn and indigestion. If symptoms persist for more than two weeks, consult a doctor.
* **Q: Can I take this with other medications?** A:  Consult your doctor or pharmacist to avoid potential interactions.
* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store as directed on the product label, typically in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture.
* **Q: What should I do if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it is almost time for the next dose. Do not double the dose.
* **Q: Is this medication addictive?** A: No, this medication is not addictive.
