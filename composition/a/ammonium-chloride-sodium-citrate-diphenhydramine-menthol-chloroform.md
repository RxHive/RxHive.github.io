**Individual Component Information (not to be combined):**


* **Ammonium Chloride:** This is an expectorant, meaning it helps thin and loosen mucus in the lungs, making it easier to cough up. It is sometimes used to treat respiratory conditions involving excessive mucus production.

* **Menthol:** This is a natural compound found in mint.  It has a cooling effect on the skin and mucous membranes and is often included in topical preparations for its soothing properties to relieve minor aches and pains.

* **Chloroform:** This is a volatile, colorless, and sweet-smelling liquid. It was once used as an anesthetic, but its use is now extremely limited due to its high toxicity and potential for serious side effects, including liver and kidney damage, cardiac irregularities, and central nervous system depression.  **Its use is strongly discouraged and highly regulated.**
