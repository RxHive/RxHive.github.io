# Alpha Lipoic Acid + Benfotiamine + Calcium citrate + Chromium polynicotinate + Folic Acid + Inositol + Methylcobalamin + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Vitamin D

**Generic Name:** Alpha Lipoic Acid + Benfotiamine + Calcium citrate + Chromium polynicotinate + Folic Acid + Inositol + Methylcobalamin + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Vitamin D

**Usage:** This combination of nutrients is often used to support metabolic health and nerve function.  It doesn't treat specific diseases in the way a conventional drug might, but rather aims to address underlying nutritional deficiencies that can contribute to various conditions.  

Its primary use focuses on improving symptoms associated with peripheral neuropathy (nerve damage), particularly in individuals with diabetes. The individual components have been studied for their roles in blood sugar control, reducing oxidative stress, and improving nerve health.  Secondary uses might include supporting cognitive function, managing symptoms of metabolic syndrome (a cluster of conditions like high blood sugar, high blood pressure, and excess abdominal fat), and potentially improving energy levels.  It's important to note that the effectiveness for these secondary uses varies and requires more research.  This combination is often considered a supportive therapy and shouldn't replace conventional medical treatment.


**Dosage:**  There's no universally standardized dosage for this specific combination as products vary in their formulation and the concentration of each ingredient.  Dosage instructions will always be found on the product label.  It's crucial to follow the recommended dosage precisely.  Always consult a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage based on your individual needs and health condition, especially if you have pre-existing conditions or are taking other medications.  This combination is typically available as oral tablets or capsules.  There are no injectable forms of this specific combination.


**Side Effects:**  This combination generally has a good safety profile, but potential side effects can include:

*   Mild gastrointestinal upset (nausea, diarrhea, constipation)
*   Skin rash or itching
*   Headache
*   Low blood sugar (hypoglycemia) - especially relevant for individuals with diabetes or those taking blood sugar-lowering medication.
*   Changes in taste or smell

Rare but more serious side effects are possible.  Seek immediate medical attention if you experience any severe allergic reaction (e.g., difficulty breathing, swelling of the face or throat) or other concerning symptoms.


**How it Works:**  This supplement works synergistically through the actions of its individual components.  For example:

*   **Alpha-lipoic acid (ALA):** A powerful antioxidant that helps protect cells from damage.
*   **Benfotiamine:** A fat-soluble form of thiamine (vitamin B1) that may improve glucose metabolism and nerve function.
*   **Calcium citrate:** Provides calcium, essential for bone health and numerous metabolic processes.
*   **Chromium polynicotinate:** May enhance insulin sensitivity, helping regulate blood sugar levels.
*   **Folic acid:** Crucial for cell growth and development, DNA synthesis, and red blood cell production.
*   **Inositol:** Plays a role in cell signaling and may have benefits for mood and metabolic function.
*   **Methylcobalamin:** An active form of vitamin B12, important for nerve function and red blood cell production.
*   **Pyridoxine hydrochloride (vitamin B6):** Involved in various metabolic processes, including the synthesis of neurotransmitters.
*   **Vitamin D:** Essential for calcium absorption, bone health, and immune function.


By working together, these nutrients aim to support healthy metabolic function, protect nerve cells from damage, and improve overall well-being.


**Precautions:**

*   **Interactions:** This combination may interact with certain medications, particularly those affecting blood sugar, blood clotting, or nerve function.  Always inform your doctor or pharmacist of all medications and supplements you are taking.
*   **Pregnant and breastfeeding women:** Consult your doctor before taking this supplement, as the safety during pregnancy and breastfeeding hasn't been fully established for all components.
*   **Individuals with diabetes:** Carefully monitor your blood sugar levels, as this combination may lower blood sugar.
*   **Kidney or liver disease:** Use caution and consult your healthcare provider before use, as some components may need adjustment.
*   **Allergies:** Check the ingredient list for potential allergens.
