# Amlodipine + Lisinopril

**Generic Name:** Amlodipine + Lisinopril

**Usage:**

Amlodipine + Lisinopril is a combination medication used primarily to treat high blood pressure (hypertension).  It's particularly helpful for people whose blood pressure isn't adequately controlled with either amlodipine or lisinopril alone.  Both medications work in different ways to lower blood pressure, making the combination more effective than using either drug individually in many cases.  This combination can also be used to treat certain types of heart failure, helping to reduce symptoms and improve the heart's function.  The specific condition being treated will determine the prescribed dosage and monitoring plan.


**Dosage:**

Dosage is determined by a healthcare professional and varies significantly depending on individual health needs and response to treatment.  It's crucial *never* to adjust the dosage without consulting a doctor. This combination medication is typically available in tablet form.  There is no standard pediatric formulation of this specific combination; amlodipine and lisinopril are sometimes prescribed individually for children in specific circumstances under strict medical supervision.  Typical adult starting doses range from 5mg/10mg to 10mg/20mg (amlodipine/lisinopril) taken once daily.  The dosage may be adjusted gradually as needed under medical guidance.

**Side Effects:**

Common side effects can include:

* Dizziness
* Headache
* Fatigue
* Swelling in ankles and feet
* Dry cough (often related to lisinopril)
* Nausea
* Lightheadedness


Less common but serious side effects require immediate medical attention and include:

* Swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat (angioedema – a potentially life-threatening allergic reaction)
* Irregular heartbeat
* Chest pain
* Difficulty breathing
* Yellowing of skin or eyes (jaundice)


If you experience any unusual or concerning side effects, contact your healthcare provider immediately.

**How it Works:**

This combination medication works through the synergistic action of its two components:

* **Amlodipine:** This is a calcium channel blocker. It relaxes blood vessels, making it easier for blood to flow, thereby lowering blood pressure.

* **Lisinopril:** This is an ACE inhibitor (angiotensin-converting enzyme inhibitor). It works by blocking the production of a hormone that narrows blood vessels, thus also contributing to lower blood pressure and improving heart function in heart failure.

By combining these two mechanisms, amlodipine + lisinopril offers a more comprehensive approach to blood pressure control and heart failure management.

**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:**  This medication should not be used by individuals with a history of angioedema related to ACE inhibitors, or those with severe kidney problems or very low blood pressure.
* **Drug Interactions:**  Numerous drugs can interact with amlodipine or lisinopril.  It's vital to inform your doctor about all medications, supplements, and herbal remedies you are taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:** This medication should be used with extreme caution during pregnancy and breastfeeding.  The risks and benefits need to be carefully weighed by a healthcare professional.
* **Older Adults and Children:**  Dosage adjustments may be necessary for older adults due to potential age-related changes in kidney function.  As mentioned earlier, this particular combination isn't generally prescribed to children.
* **Kidney and Liver Disease:** Individuals with kidney or liver impairment may require lower doses or more frequent monitoring.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: Can I stop taking this medication suddenly?** A: No, abruptly stopping this medication can be harmful.  Always taper off the medication under the supervision of your healthcare provider.

* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store this medication in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture, and out of the reach of children.

* **Q: What if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it's nearly time for your next dose.  Don't take a double dose to make up for a missed one.

* **Q: Is it safe to drink alcohol while taking this medication?** A:  Excessive alcohol consumption can negatively impact blood pressure. Moderate alcohol consumption should be discussed with your doctor.

* **Q: Will this medication cure my high blood pressure?** A: No, this medication manages high blood pressure.  Lifestyle changes such as diet and exercise are also crucial for long-term control.
