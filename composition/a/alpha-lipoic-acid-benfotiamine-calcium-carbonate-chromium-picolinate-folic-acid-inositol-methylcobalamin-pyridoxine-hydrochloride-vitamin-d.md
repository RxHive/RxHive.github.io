# Alpha Lipoic Acid + Benfotiamine + Calcium Carbonate + Chromium Picolinate + Folic Acid + Inositol + Methylcobalamin + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Vitamin D

**Generic Name:** Alpha Lipoic Acid + Benfotiamine + Calcium Carbonate + Chromium Picolinate + Folic Acid + Inositol + Methylcobalamin + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Vitamin D

**Usage:** This combination of nutrients is not typically prescribed as a single medication for a specific disease.  Instead, it represents a synergistic blend of compounds often found in supplements aimed at supporting metabolic health, nerve function, and overall well-being.  Each component plays a distinct role:

* **Alpha-lipoic acid (ALA):** A potent antioxidant that may help protect cells from damage. It's sometimes used to support nerve function in conditions like diabetic neuropathy.
* **Benfotiamine:** A fat-soluble form of vitamin B1 (thiamine), potentially more effective at reaching tissues than regular thiamine.  It's also studied for its role in managing diabetic complications.
* **Calcium Carbonate:** A common source of calcium, essential for bone health, muscle function, and nerve transmission.
* **Chromium Picolinate:** A form of chromium, a trace mineral that may help improve insulin sensitivity and regulate blood sugar levels.
* **Folic Acid (Vitamin B9):** Crucial for cell growth and development, especially important during pregnancy.  Deficiencies can lead to anemia and birth defects.
* **Inositol:** A naturally occurring substance involved in numerous cellular processes, including insulin signaling and nerve function. It's sometimes used to address symptoms of anxiety and polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS).
* **Methylcobalamin:** A form of vitamin B12, vital for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Deficiencies can lead to anemia and neurological problems.
* **Pyridoxine Hydrochloride (Vitamin B6):** Involved in numerous metabolic processes, including the synthesis of neurotransmitters and red blood cells.  Deficiencies can cause anemia and neurological symptoms.
* **Vitamin D:** Essential for calcium absorption, bone health, and immune function.  Deficiencies are common and linked to various health issues.

The combination is often marketed to support overall health, particularly in individuals with conditions like diabetes or those seeking to improve metabolic function and nerve health.  However, it's vital to note this is not a treatment for these conditions, but rather a nutritional support approach.  Always consult a healthcare professional before using this supplement, especially if you have pre-existing medical conditions or are taking other medications.
