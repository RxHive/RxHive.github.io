# Amoxapine

**Generic Name: Amoxapine**

**Usage:**

Amoxapine is an antidepressant medication primarily used to treat **major depressive disorder (MDD)**.  It's classified as a tetracyclic antidepressant, meaning it has a slightly different chemical structure than the more common tricyclic antidepressants.  While its primary use is for MDD, it may also be used, though less commonly and often off-label, to help manage symptoms of **schizophrenia** where depression coexists.  The effectiveness and appropriateness of amoxapine for schizophrenia require careful consideration by a psychiatrist due to potential side effects.
