# Hydrochlorothiazide + Olmesartan Medoxomil

**Generic Name:** Hydrochlorothiazide + Olmesartan Medoxomil

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to treat hypertension (high blood pressure).  It's a particularly effective treatment for patients whose blood pressure isn't adequately controlled with either hydrochlorothiazide or olmesartan medoxomil alone.  Hydrochlorothiazide is a diuretic (water pill) that helps your body get rid of excess salt and water, lowering blood pressure. Olmesartan medoxomil is an angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB) that relaxes blood vessels, further reducing blood pressure.  The combined action of these two medications provides a more potent and comprehensive approach to blood pressure management than either drug in isolation.  While not its primary use, this combination may sometimes be considered for patients with heart failure, but this is usually determined on a case-by-case basis by a cardiologist.

**Dosage:**  Dosage is individualized and determined by a physician based on your specific health needs and response to the medication.  Generally, for adults, the starting dose is one tablet containing a specific combination of hydrochlorothiazide and olmesartan medoxomil (e.g., 12.5mg/40mg or 25mg/80mg). The doctor may adjust the dosage upward or downward based on blood pressure readings and individual tolerance.  This medication is typically available as a tablet and is taken orally once daily.  **This medication is not recommended for children.**  The elderly may require a lower starting dose due to age-related changes in kidney function.


**Side Effects:** Common side effects can include:

* Dizziness
* Lightheadedness
* Fatigue
* Headache
* Dry mouth
* Muscle cramps
* Nausea
* Diarrhea
* Changes in potassium levels (hypokalemia or hyperkalemia)

Less common, but potentially serious side effects include:

* Angioedema (swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat) - Seek immediate medical attention if this occurs.
* Allergic reactions (rash, itching, difficulty breathing)
* Low blood pressure (hypotension)
* Kidney problems
* Increased blood sugar levels (hyperglycemia)


If you experience any side effects, especially those listed as serious, consult your doctor or healthcare provider immediately.


**How it Works:** Hydrochlorothiazide increases the excretion of sodium and water from the body by acting on the kidneys. This reduces blood volume and consequently, blood pressure. Olmesartan medoxomil blocks the action of angiotensin II, a hormone that narrows blood vessels.  By blocking angiotensin II, olmesartan medoxomil causes blood vessels to relax and widen, further reducing blood pressure.  The combined effect of these two mechanisms results in a more significant and sustained decrease in blood pressure.

**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:**  This medication is contraindicated in patients with severe kidney disease, severe liver disease, an allergy to sulfonamide drugs (hydrochlorothiazide is a sulfonamide), or those who are already taking aliskiren (a medication used to treat high blood pressure).
* **Drug Interactions:**  This medication can interact with many other drugs, including potassium supplements, ACE inhibitors, nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), and lithium.  Inform your doctor of all medications you are currently taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:**  This medication is generally not recommended during pregnancy and breastfeeding. Discuss the risks and benefits with your doctor before using this medication if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
* **Elderly:**  Older adults may be more sensitive to the side effects of this medication, requiring a lower starting dose and close monitoring.
* **Dehydration:**  Avoid dehydration, as it can increase the risk of low blood pressure.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: Can I stop taking this medication suddenly?** A: No.  Stopping abruptly can lead to a sudden increase in blood pressure.  Always consult your doctor before stopping or changing your medication.
* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store at room temperature, away from moisture and heat.
* **Q: What should I do if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it's almost time for your next dose.  Do not double the dose.
* **Q: Can I drink alcohol while taking this medication?** A: Excessive alcohol consumption can lower blood pressure and increase the risk of side effects.  Moderate alcohol consumption should be discussed with your doctor.
* **Q:  Does this medication cause weight gain?** A: While not a common major side effect, some individuals may experience minor weight changes.  This should be discussed with your doctor if it is a concern.
