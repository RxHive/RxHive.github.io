# Diloxanide + Ornidazole + Simethicone

**Generic Name:** Diloxanide + Ornidazole + Simethicone

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to treat intestinal infections caused by certain parasites, specifically amoebiasis (infection with *Entamoeba histolytica*) and giardiasis (infection with *Giardia lamblia*).  Diloxanide furoate targets *Entamoeba histolytica*, ornidazole tackles both *Entamoeba histolytica* and *Giardia lamblia*, and simethicone helps relieve associated symptoms like bloating and gas.  While its primary use is in treating these parasitic infections, it's crucial to understand that this medication should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.  Self-medication can be dangerous.

**Dosage:** Dosage varies depending on age, weight, and the severity of the infection.  A healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dosage.  Generally, this medication comes in tablet form.  Specific instructions will be provided on the prescription label. It's vital to follow these instructions precisely.  There isn't a standard universally accepted pediatric dosage readily available in public sources; therefore, a doctor's prescription is paramount for children.

**Side Effects:**  The combination of these medications can lead to various side effects, although not everyone experiences them.

* **Common Side Effects:** Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal cramps, headache, dizziness, metallic taste in the mouth.
* **Less Common but Serious Side Effects:**  Severe allergic reactions (rash, hives, difficulty breathing, swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat), numbness or tingling in the extremities, changes in vision or hearing.

**Note:**  If you experience any of these side effects, particularly the serious ones, seek immediate medical attention.


**How it Works:** Diloxanide furoate works by interfering with the growth and reproduction of *Entamoeba histolytica*. Ornidazole is an anti-parasitic drug that kills *Entamoeba histolytica* and *Giardia lamblia* by damaging their DNA.  Simethicone is an anti-flatulent agent; it helps reduce gas bubbles in the intestines, alleviating symptoms of bloating and discomfort often associated with intestinal infections.

**Popular Brand Names (India):**  Specific brand names vary widely by region and manufacturer.  It is best to consult a pharmacist or doctor in India for locally available brand names.

**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** This medication should not be used by individuals with known hypersensitivity (allergy) to diloxanide furoate, ornidazole, simethicone, or any of the inactive ingredients.
* **Drug Interactions:**  Inform your doctor about all medications you are taking, including over-the-counter drugs, herbal remedies, and supplements, as interactions are possible.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:**  Use during pregnancy and breastfeeding should only be considered if the potential benefits outweigh the risks.  Always consult a doctor before using this medication if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.
* **Children and Older Adults:**  Dosage adjustments may be necessary for children and older adults.  A doctor's prescription is crucial in these groups.
* **Liver or Kidney Disease:** Individuals with liver or kidney disease should use this medication with caution and under close medical supervision, as it might be metabolized or excreted differently.

**FAQs:**

* **Q: How long does it take to work?**  A:  The duration of treatment and the time it takes to see improvement vary depending on the individual and the severity of the infection.  Follow your doctor's prescribed treatment course.
* **Q: Can I drink alcohol while taking this medication?** A:  No. Alcohol can interact negatively with ornidazole and potentially worsen side effects.
* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store at room temperature, away from moisture and direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
* **Q: What should I do if I miss a dose?** A: Take the missed dose as soon as you remember, unless it is almost time for your next dose. Do not double the dose.
* **Q: Is this medication safe for long-term use?** A: This medication is not intended for long-term use.  Follow your doctor's instructions regarding the duration of treatment.
