# Diclofenac + Paracetamol + Chlorpheniramine Maleate + Magnesium Trisilicate

**Generic Name:** Diclofenac + Paracetamol + Chlorpheniramine Maleate + Magnesium Trisilicate

**Usage:** This combination medication is typically used to provide relief from symptoms associated with the common cold, flu, and various other conditions involving pain, fever, and allergic reactions.  Let's break down each component's role:

* **Diclofenac:** A non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that reduces pain and inflammation. It's effective against headaches, muscle aches, and joint pain stemming from conditions like arthritis or injury.
* **Paracetamol (Acetaminophen):** An analgesic and antipyretic that reduces fever and relieves mild to moderate pain.  It works differently from NSAIDs, targeting pain pathways in the brain.
