**Generic Name:** Diphenhydramine + Guaifenesin + Bromhexine + Ammonium Chloride

**Usage:** This combination medication is typically used to relieve symptoms associated with the common cold, bronchitis, and other upper respiratory tract infections.  It's a multi-ingredient formulation designed to address several aspects of these conditions simultaneously.
