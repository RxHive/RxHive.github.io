# Salicylic Acid + Dithranol + Coal Tar

**Generic Name:** Salicylic Acid + Dithranol + Coal Tar

**Usage:** This combination of salicylic acid, dithranol, and coal tar is primarily used in the topical treatment of psoriasis, a chronic skin condition characterized by inflamed, scaly patches.  Each ingredient contributes to its effectiveness:

* **Salicylic acid:** Acts as a keratolytic, meaning it helps soften and loosen the scales and plaques characteristic of psoriasis, making them easier to remove.
* **Dithranol (anthralin):**  Is a potent anti-inflammatory and antiproliferative agent that slows down the rapid skin cell growth associated with psoriasis.
* **Coal tar:** Possesses anti-inflammatory and antiproliferative properties, similar to dithranol, and also helps to reduce itching and scaling.

While primarily used for psoriasis, this combination may sometimes be considered for other skin conditions involving excessive scaling or inflammation, though this is less common and should only be done under strict medical supervision.  The specific suitability for conditions other than psoriasis will depend on individual factors and the physician's assessment.


**Dosage:**  There is no standardized dosage for this combination medication as formulations vary significantly depending on the manufacturer and the specific concentration of each active ingredient.  Dosage and application instructions are always provided with the specific product.  It's crucial to follow these instructions precisely.  Generally, application involves a thin layer to the affected area, often starting with low-concentration formulations and gradually increasing the concentration as tolerated. The frequency of application (e.g., once daily, twice daily) is also dictated by the prescription.  Children's dosage must be determined by a physician and is usually based on the affected area and the child's age and weight.  This medication is for external use only; oral ingestion is extremely dangerous.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:** Skin irritation (redness, burning, itching), dryness, staining of skin and clothing.
* **Less Common but Serious:**  Severe allergic reactions (rash, hives, swelling, difficulty breathing), increased sensitivity to sunlight (photosensitivity).

If you experience any adverse effects, especially severe reactions, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional immediately.


**How it Works:** The combined action of salicylic acid, dithranol, and coal tar addresses the key features of psoriasis. Salicylic acid helps remove the thickened, scaly skin. Dithranol and coal tar slow down the rapid skin cell turnover that contributes to the raised, inflamed plaques.  Together, they reduce inflammation, scaling, and itching associated with the condition.


**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** This medication is contraindicated in individuals with known hypersensitivity (allergy) to salicylic acid, dithranol, coal tar, or any other components of the formulation.
* **Drug Interactions:**  Interactions with other medications are not commonly documented for this topical combination, but it's advisable to inform your doctor or pharmacist of all medications you are currently taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:** The safety of this combination during pregnancy and breastfeeding has not been fully established. Consult a doctor before using this medication if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to become pregnant.
* **Children and Elderly:**  Use in children and the elderly requires careful monitoring by a physician due to potential increased sensitivity to the medication.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: How long does it take to see results?**  A: The time it takes to see improvement varies depending on the severity of psoriasis and individual response.  You may see some improvement within weeks, but consistent treatment is usually necessary for several months.

* **Q: Can I use this medication on my face?** A:  Generally, it is not recommended to use this combination on the face, unless specifically instructed by a dermatologist, due to the potential for significant irritation.

* **Q: How should I store this medication?** A: Store as directed on the product label, usually at room temperature, away from moisture and direct sunlight.

* **Q: What should I do if I accidentally ingest this medication?** A: Seek immediate medical attention.  Ingestion is dangerous and can cause serious side effects.
