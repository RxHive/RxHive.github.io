# Tadalafil + Dapoxetine

**Generic Name: Tadalafil + Dapoxetine**

**Usage:**

Tadalafil + Dapoxetine is a combination medication primarily used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED) and premature ejaculation (PE) in adult men.  Tadalafil, a phosphodiesterase-5 (PDE5) inhibitor, addresses ED by increasing blood flow to the penis, facilitating an erection. Dapoxetine, a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI), helps delay ejaculation by affecting the neurotransmitters involved in the ejaculatory reflex.  While the primary use is combined treatment of ED and PE, it's crucial to understand that this is a prescription medication and should only be used under the strict guidance of a doctor.  This combination is not intended for use in women or children.

**Dosage:**

Dosage is determined by a healthcare professional based on individual needs and health status.  There is no standardized "child" dosage, as this medication is not approved for use in children.  Typical adult dosage usually involves taking a single tablet, as directed by a physician, before anticipated sexual activity. The specific dosage of Tadalafil and Dapoxetine within the combination tablet will vary depending on the formulation.  It is crucial to follow the prescribed dosage precisely; do not exceed the recommended dose.  Different formulations may be available as tablets, but other forms like syrups or injections are not typically available for this combination.  Improper use can lead to adverse effects.

**Side Effects:**

Common side effects can include:

* Headache
* Flushing (redness of the face, neck, or chest)
* Nasal congestion
* Upset stomach
* Back pain
* Muscle aches
* Dizziness
* Vision changes (blurred vision, changes in color perception)


Less common, but serious side effects may include:

* Prolonged erection (priapism) – Seek immediate medical attention if an erection lasts longer than four hours.
* Sudden decrease or loss of vision
* Sudden decrease or loss of hearing
* Severe allergic reactions (anaphylaxis) – Seek immediate medical attention if you experience symptoms such as hives, swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat,
