# Tegaserod

**Generic Name: Tegaserod**

**Usage:**

Tegaserod is a medication primarily used to treat Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) with constipation (IBS-C).  IBS is a chronic condition affecting the large intestine, causing symptoms like abdominal pain, bloating, and infrequent, difficult bowel movements. Tegaserod works by stimulating the movement of the intestines, helping to relieve these symptoms.  While its primary use is in IBS-C, it's important to note that Tegaserod is no longer widely available in many countries due to concerns about potential cardiovascular side effects.  Therefore, its use should be discussed thoroughly with a doctor.  Any mention of secondary uses or off-label applications should be addressed only within the context of a consultation with a healthcare professional.

**How it Works:**

Tegaserod is a selective serotonin 5-HT4 receptor agonist.  In simpler terms, it works by stimulating specific receptors in the gut that increase the movement of the intestines (motility) and help facilitate the passage of stool. This action relieves the constipation associated with IBS-C.
