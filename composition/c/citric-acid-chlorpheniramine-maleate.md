# Citric Acid + Chlorpheniramine Maleate

**Generic Name:** Citric Acid + Chlorpheniramine Maleate

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to relieve symptoms associated with the common cold, allergies, and hay fever.  Citric acid acts as a flavoring agent and buffering agent, making the medication more palatable and helping to maintain its stability.
