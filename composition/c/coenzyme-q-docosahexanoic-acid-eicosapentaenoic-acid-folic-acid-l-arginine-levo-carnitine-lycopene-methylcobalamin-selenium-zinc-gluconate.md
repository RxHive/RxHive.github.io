**Usage:**

This combination of ingredients targets several aspects of health and well-being, often focusing on cardiovascular health, cognitive function, and antioxidant protection. Let's break down the individual components and their roles:

* **Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10):**  Plays a vital role in energy production within cells.  It's often used to support heart health and may help reduce oxidative stress.

* **DHA and EPA (Omega-3 Fatty Acids):**  Essential fatty acids with well-established benefits for heart health, brain function, and reducing inflammation.

* **Folic Acid (Vitamin B9):**  Crucial for cell growth and development, particularly important during pregnancy to prevent neural tube defects.  Also plays a role in red blood cell formation.

* **L-Arginine:** An amino acid that helps produce nitric oxide, a molecule that relaxes blood vessels and improves blood flow.  May be used to support cardiovascular health.

* **Levo-carnitine:**  Plays a role in energy production, particularly in the heart and muscles.  Sometimes used to improve physical performance and may have benefits for people with certain heart conditions.

* **Lycopene:**  A powerful antioxidant found in tomatoes and other red fruits.  It may help protect against cellular damage and is associated with reduced risk of certain cancers and heart disease.

* **Methylcobalamin (Vitamin B12):**  Essential for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Deficiencies can lead to neurological problems and anemia.

* **Selenium:** A trace mineral that acts as an antioxidant and plays a role in thyroid hormone metabolism and immune function.

* **Zinc Gluconate:**  Zinc is essential for immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.
