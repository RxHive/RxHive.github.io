# Coenzyme Q + Lycopene + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate + Selenium + Folic Acid + L-Arginine

**Usage:**

The primary intended use of this combination is generally to support antioxidant defense, promote cardiovascular health, and contribute to overall well-being.  Each individual component plays a specific role:

* **Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10):**  An antioxidant that plays a crucial role in energy production within cells.  It's sometimes used to support heart health and may help manage symptoms of heart failure.
* **Lycopene:** A potent carotenoid antioxidant found in tomatoes and other red fruits. It's associated with potential benefits for prostate health and may contribute to reducing the risk of certain cancers.
* **Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate:**  Zinc is an essential mineral involved in numerous bodily functions, including immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.
* **Selenium:** A trace mineral acting as an antioxidant and playing a role in thyroid hormone metabolism and immune function.
* **Folic Acid:** A B vitamin crucial for cell growth and development, especially during pregnancy.  It's vital for preventing neural tube defects in developing fetuses.
* **L-Arginine:** An amino acid that's a precursor to nitric oxide, a molecule that helps relax blood vessels and improve blood flow.


It's crucial to understand that this combination isn't a treatment for any specific disease but rather a nutritional approach potentially supporting overall health parameters.  Claims regarding specific disease treatment should be carefully evaluated and discussed with a healthcare professional.
