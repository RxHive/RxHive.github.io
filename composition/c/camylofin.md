Its primary use is in the management of acute and chronic muscle spasms, often seen in conditions like back pain, neck pain, and other forms of musculoskeletal pain where muscle stiffness and spasm are contributing factors. While not its primary indication, it may sometimes be used as an adjunctive therapy (meaning used in addition to other treatments) for certain types of neurological conditions involving muscle spasms.
