# Cyanocobalamin + Elemental Iron + Folic Acid

**Generic Name:** Cyanocobalamin + Elemental Iron + Folic Acid

**Usage:** This combination medication is primarily used to treat and prevent deficiencies in vitamin B12 (cyanocobalamin), iron, and folate (folic acid).  These deficiencies can lead to various health problems, most notably anemia
