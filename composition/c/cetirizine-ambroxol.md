# Cetirizine + Ambroxol

**Generic Name: Cetirizine + Ambroxol**

**Usage:**

Cetirizine + Ambroxol is a combination medication used to treat symptoms associated with the common cold, flu, and other upper respiratory tract infections.  It's a particularly helpful treatment when symptoms include both allergic reactions and mucus production.

* **Primary Use:**  Relief of symptoms like runny nose, sneezing, itchy eyes, and cough associated with allergies (allergic rhinitis) and the common cold.  The cetirizine component targets the allergic response, while the ambroxol component helps thin and loosen mucus, making it easier to cough up.

* **Secondary Uses:** While primarily used for the above, it may sometimes be considered for symptom relief in other conditions involving cough and allergic manifestations, but only under the direct guidance of a doctor.  It's crucial to understand this combination isn't a cure for the underlying infection but focuses on symptomatic relief.


**Dosage:**

Dosage varies depending on the specific formulation (tablet, syrup), the patient's age and weight, and the severity of symptoms.  Always follow the instructions provided by your doctor or pharmacist.  This information is for general knowledge and should not replace professional medical advice.  **Never self-medicate.**

* **Adults:**  Typical dosages might range from one to two tablets or a specified amount of syrup, usually once or twice daily.

* **Children:** Dosage for children is strictly weight-based and determined by a healthcare professional.  Syrup formulations are commonly used for children, with dosage adjusted according to their age and weight.  Never give adult medication to children.

* **Specific Instructions:**  Take the medication as directed. It's usually taken with or without food.  Do not exceed the recommended dose.

**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Drowsiness, headache, dry mouth, nausea, dizziness, stomach upset.

* **Less Common but Serious:**  Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling, difficulty breathing), fast heartbeat, seizures.

* **Important Note:** If you experience any serious or persistent side effects, stop taking the medication and seek immediate medical attention.
