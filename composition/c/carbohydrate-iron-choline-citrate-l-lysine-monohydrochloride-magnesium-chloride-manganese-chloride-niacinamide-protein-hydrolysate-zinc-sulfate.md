# Carbohydrate + Iron Choline Citrate + L-Lysine Monohydrochloride + Magnesium Chloride + Manganese Chloride + Niacinamide + Protein Hydrolysate + Zinc Sulfate

**Usage:**

The primary intended use is nutritional supplementation.  Each component serves a specific role:

* **Carbohydrate:** Provides energy.
* **Iron Choline Citrate:**  Iron is crucial for red blood cell production and oxygen transport; choline contributes to brain function and cell membrane health.
* **L-Lysine Monohydrochloride:** An essential amino acid important for protein synthesis, calcium absorption, and immune function.
* **Magnesium Chloride:**  Magnesium plays a role in numerous bodily functions, including muscle and nerve function, blood sugar control, and blood pressure regulation.
* **Manganese Chloride:** A trace mineral involved in bone health, wound healing, and metabolism.
* **Niacinamide (Vitamin B3):**  Important for energy metabolism, DNA repair, and skin health.
* **Protein Hydrolysate:** A readily digestible source of protein, supplying essential amino acids for tissue repair and growth.
* **Zinc Sulfate:** Zinc is vital for immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.


This combination might be suggested to support recovery from illness, address nutritional deficiencies (e.g., anemia, protein deficiency), or enhance overall nutritional intake in individuals with increased needs (e.g., athletes, pregnant women). However, it's crucial to remember that this is not a replacement for a balanced diet.
