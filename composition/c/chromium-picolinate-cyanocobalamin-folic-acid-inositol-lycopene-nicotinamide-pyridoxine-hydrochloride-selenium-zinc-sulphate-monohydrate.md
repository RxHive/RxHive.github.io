# Chromium Picolinate + Cyanocobalamin + Folic Acid + Inositol + Lycopene + Nicotinamide + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Selenium + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Generic Name:** Chromium Picolinate + Cyanocobalamin + Folic Acid + Inositol + Lycopene + Nicotinamide + Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Selenium + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Usage:** This combination of nutrients is not a single "drug" with FDA approval for treating specific medical conditions. Instead, it's a multivitamin and mineral supplement designed to address potential deficiencies in several essential micronutrients.  Each component plays a role in various bodily functions:

* **Chromium Picolinate:** Contributes to glucose metabolism and may help regulate blood sugar levels.  Some studies suggest a potential role in weight management, but more research is needed.
* **Cyanocobalamin (Vitamin B12):** Crucial for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Deficiencies can lead to anemia, nerve damage, and fatigue.
* **Folic Acid (Vitamin B9):** Essential for cell growth and development, particularly important during pregnancy to prevent neural tube defects.  Plays a vital role in DNA synthesis and red blood cell production.
* **Inositol:** A naturally occurring compound involved in various cellular processes, including insulin signaling and nerve function. Some research suggests potential benefits for mood disorders and metabolic health, but more robust studies are required.
* **Lycopene:** A potent antioxidant found in tomatoes and other red fruits.  May offer protection against cellular damage and some chronic diseases.  Research on its effectiveness is ongoing.
* **Nicotinamide (Vitamin B3):**  Important for energy production, DNA repair, and cell signaling. Deficiencies are rare but can lead to pellagra.
* **Pyridoxine Hydrochloride (Vitamin B6):**  Involved in numerous metabolic processes, including protein metabolism, neurotransmitter synthesis, and red blood cell formation.
* **Selenium:** An antioxidant mineral that contributes to immune function and thyroid hormone metabolism.
* **Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate:**  Essential for immune function, wound healing, cell growth, and enzyme activity.  Deficiencies can lead to impaired immune function and growth retardation.


This combination is primarily used as a nutritional supplement to address suspected or confirmed deficiencies in these vitamins and minerals.  It's often marketed for overall health and well-being, but it's not a treatment for specific diseases.


**Dosage:** There's no standardized dosage for this specific combination as formulations vary widely.  Dosage instructions will be found on the product label.  Always follow the manufacturer's recommendations.  Individual needs may vary depending on age, health status, and dietary intake.  Consult a healthcare professional to determine the appropriate dosage based on your individual needs.  It's crucial to remember that exceeding recommended doses may lead to adverse effects.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Generally, this combination is well-tolerated. However, mild digestive upset (nausea, diarrhea, constipation) may occur.
* **Less Common but Serious:** Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling),  more serious reactions are rare but possible.


If you experience any adverse effects, stop taking the supplement and consult a healthcare professional immediately.


**How it Works:** Each component in this supplement works independently to support its respective bodily functions, contributing to overall health and well-being by addressing potential deficiencies.  For example, vitamin B12 helps in red blood cell formation, while zinc supports immune function.  The combined effect aims for synergistic improvements in various aspects of health.
