# Calcium Citrate Malate + Magnesium Oxide + Methylcobalamin + Vitamin D + Vitamin K + Zinc Oxide

**Generic Name:** Calcium Citrate Malate + Magnesium Oxide + Methylcobalamin + Vitamin D + Vitamin K + Zinc Oxide

**Usage:** This combination supplement is primarily used to address nutritional deficiencies in calcium, magnesium, vitamin B12 (methylcobalamin), vitamin D, vitamin K, and zinc.  It's not a treatment for specific diseases but rather a supportive measure to maintain healthy levels of these essential nutrients.  The individual nutrients within this combination play vital roles in various bodily functions:

* **Calcium & Magnesium:** Crucial for strong bones and teeth, muscle function, nerve transmission, and blood clotting. Deficiencies can lead to osteoporosis, muscle cramps, and other health problems.
* **Vitamin B12 (Methylcobalamin):** Essential for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Deficiency can cause anemia, nerve damage (peripheral neuropathy), and fatigue.
* **Vitamin D:**  Supports calcium absorption, bone health, immune function, and overall well-being. Deficiency can contribute to rickets in children and osteomalacia/osteoporosis in adults.
* **Vitamin K:** Important for blood clotting and bone health. Deficiency can increase bleeding risk.
* **Zinc:** Plays a role in immune function, wound healing, cell growth, and numerous enzyme activities. Deficiency can weaken the immune system and impair growth.

This combination supplement might be recommended for individuals with suspected or confirmed deficiencies in any of these nutrients, particularly those at higher risk (e.g., older adults, pregnant women, vegans/vegetarians).  However, it's crucial to remember that this is not a substitute for a balanced diet and lifestyle.


**Dosage:**  Dosage varies significantly depending on the specific product and individual needs.  A healthcare professional will determine the appropriate dosage based on factors such as age, weight, overall health, and the severity of any diagnosed deficiency.  Generally, the supplement is available in tablet form, and instructions will be provided on the product packaging.  **Always follow the instructions on the label or as directed by your doctor.**  There is no standard dosage for children as it depends heavily on the child's age, weight, and specific needs, making it crucial to consult a pediatrician.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:** Mild gastrointestinal upset (e.g., nausea, constipation, diarrhea), headache.
* **Less Common but Serious:**  Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling, difficulty breathing), kidney stones (with high calcium intake),  interactions with other medications.


**How it Works:** This combination supplement works by providing the body with the necessary nutrients it may be lacking. Each nutrient plays a specific role in various metabolic pathways and bodily processes, helping to restore and maintain optimal function.  For instance, vitamin D aids calcium absorption, while vitamin K is crucial for blood clotting, and zinc supports immune function.  The combined effect provides comprehensive nutritional support.

**Popular Brand Names (India):**  Specific brand names will vary depending on availability and local regulations. It's best to check with local pharmacies or healthcare providers for available options.


**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** Individuals with certain kidney conditions, hypercalcemia (high blood calcium), hypermagnesemia (high blood magnesium), or known allergies to any of the ingredients should avoid this supplement.
* **Drug Interactions:** This supplement can interact with certain medications, such as some diuretics, antibiotics, and heart medications.  Always inform your doctor or pharmacist about all medications and supplements you are taking.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:** Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor before taking this supplement, as the recommended dosage may differ.
* **Children and Older Adults:**  Dosage adjustments might be necessary for children and older adults based on their individual needs and health conditions.  Always seek professional guidance.
* **Storage:** Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: Can I take this supplement without a doctor's recommendation?** A: While it's generally considered safe for many, it's best to consult a doctor, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking other medications.
* **Q: What should I do if I experience side effects?** A: Stop taking the supplement and consult your doctor immediately.
* **Q: How long should I take this supplement?** A: The duration of use depends on your individual needs and should be determined by your doctor.
* **Q: Is this supplement suitable for vegans/vegetarians?** A:  Many versions are suitable, but check the product label to ensure it's appropriate for your dietary needs (especially regarding vitamin B12 source).
* **Q: Can I take this with other supplements?** A:  Consult your doctor before combining with other supplements to avoid potential interactions.
