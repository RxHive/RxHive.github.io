**How it Works:**

Each component works through a different mechanism:

* **Clobetasol:** Suppresses inflammation by binding to specific receptors in skin cells. This reduces swelling, redness, and itching.

* **Gentamicin:** Interferes with bacterial protein synthesis, ultimately killing bacteria.

* **Clioquinol:**  Disrupts the cell membranes of fungi and bacteria, leading to their death.

* **Ketoconazole:** Inhibits the synthesis of ergosterol, a vital component of fungal cell membranes, disrupting fungal growth.

These drugs work synergistically (when prescribed in combination) to manage inflammation, bacterial infections, and fungal infections of the skin.
