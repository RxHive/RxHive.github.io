# Calcium citrate + L-Arginine + L-Lysine + Vitamin B + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Generic Name:** Calcium citrate + L-Arginine + L-Lysine + Vitamin B complex + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Usage:** This combination of ingredients is often used to support overall health and well-being, primarily focusing on areas affected by deficiencies in these nutrients.  It's not a treatment for specific diseases but rather a nutritional supplement aimed at addressing potential shortfalls.  

* **Primary Use:**  Supplementing the diet with essential nutrients.  Calcium, a vital mineral, is crucial for strong bones and teeth. L-arginine and L-lysine are amino acids involved in various bodily functions, including protein synthesis and immune support.  Vitamin B complex (which includes several B vitamins like B1, B2, B3, B6, B12 and others depending on the formulation) plays a crucial role in energy metabolism and nerve function. Zinc is a mineral essential for immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.

* **Secondary Uses:** This combination may be used to support:
    * **Improved immune function:** Through the combined action of zinc, vitamin B complex, and amino acids.
    * **Increased energy levels:**  Supported by the B vitamins.
    * **Enhanced protein synthesis:**  Due to the presence of L-arginine and L-lysine.
    * **Improved wound healing:** Assisted by zinc.

It's important to note that this supplement is not a substitute for a balanced diet or medical treatment for existing conditions.


**Dosage:**  Dosage varies significantly depending on the specific brand and formulation.  There isn't a universally standardized dosage for this combination.  Always refer to the specific product labeling for recommended dosage instructions.  Generally, tablets or capsules are taken orally, usually with a meal.  Syrups (if available) would have separate age-based dosing instructions.  **It is crucial to consult a doctor or pharmacist before starting any supplementation, especially for children, pregnant women, or individuals with pre-existing health conditions.**


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Mild gastrointestinal upset (e.g., nausea, diarrhea, stomach discomfort).
* **Less Common but Potentially Serious:** Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling).  Severe allergic reactions are rare but require immediate medical attention.
* **Other:**  Headache, dizziness.

**Note:**  This list is not exhaustive. If you experience any adverse effects, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional immediately.


**How it Works:**  Each component contributes to the overall effect:

* **Calcium citrate:** Provides readily absorbable calcium for bone health and other physiological functions.
* **L-arginine & L-lysine:**  These amino acids are building blocks of proteins and play roles in various metabolic processes, including immune function.
* **Vitamin B complex:**  These vitamins act as coenzymes in numerous metabolic pathways, crucial for energy production and nerve function.
* **Zinc sulphate monohydrate:** Supplies zinc, vital for immune function, wound healing, and cell growth.
