# Calcium Citrate Malate + Vitamin D + Vitamin K + Methylcobalamin + Magnesium Oxide + Zinc Oxide

**Generic Name:** Calcium Citrate Malate + Vitamin D + Vitamin K + Methylcobalamin + Magnesium Oxide + Zinc Oxide

**Usage:** This combination supplement is primarily used to address nutritional deficiencies and support overall bone health, particularly in individuals at risk of osteoporosis or those with inadequate dietary intake of these nutrients.  Each component plays a vital role:

* **Calcium Citrate Malate:**  A readily absorbable form of calcium, crucial for strong bones and teeth, muscle function, nerve transmission, and blood clotting.
* **Vitamin D:** Essential for calcium absorption, bone health, immune function, and overall well-being.  A deficiency can lead to weakened bones (osteomalacia and rickets) and other health problems.
* **Vitamin K:** Plays a vital role in blood clotting and bone metabolism.  It helps activate proteins essential for bone mineralization.
* **Methylcobalamin:** A form of vitamin B12, important for nerve function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. Deficiency can cause anemia and neurological problems.
* **Magnesium Oxide:**  Essential for numerous bodily functions, including muscle and nerve function, blood sugar control, and blood pressure regulation.  It also plays a role in bone health.
* **Zinc Oxide:**  Important for immune function, wound healing, cell growth, and DNA synthesis.  Deficiency can weaken the immune system and impair growth.

Secondary uses might include supporting overall health in individuals with increased nutritional demands (e.g., athletes, pregnant or breastfeeding women) or those recovering from illness. However, it's crucial to remember this is a *supplement*, not a treatment for specific diseases.  It addresses nutritional deficiencies, not underlying medical conditions.


**Dosage:**  Dosage varies greatly depending on the specific formulation and individual needs.  It's crucial to follow the instructions on the product label or as directed by a healthcare professional.  There isn't a universally applicable dosage for adults or children as needs vary based on age, health status, and dietary intake.  A doctor or registered dietitian can help determine the appropriate dosage.  The supplement is typically available in tablet form.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Constipation, diarrhea, nausea, stomach upset.
* **Less Common but Serious:**  Kidney stones (especially with pre-existing conditions), hypercalcemia (high blood calcium levels – symptoms include muscle weakness, confusion, and excessive urination), allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling).

**Note:**  If you experience any adverse effects, discontinue use and consult a healthcare provider immediately.


**How it Works:** The supplement works by providing the body with essential nutrients that it may be lacking.  Each component contributes to its specific functions: calcium strengthens bones, vitamin D aids calcium absorption, vitamin K supports bone metabolism and clotting, methylcobalamin supports nerve function and red blood cell production, magnesium supports numerous bodily functions, and zinc supports immune function and growth.  By supplying these nutrients, the supplement helps maintain optimal bodily functions and reduces the risk of deficiencies.

**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:** Individuals with hypercalcemia (high blood calcium), kidney stones, or those taking certain medications (e.g., some heart medications, antibiotics) should consult a doctor before using this supplement.
* **Drug Interactions:** This supplement can interact with certain medications. Inform your doctor or pharmacist about all medications, supplements, and herbal remedies you are taking.
* **Pregnant/Breastfeeding Women:**  Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctor before using this supplement, as the appropriate dosage may differ.
* **Children:**  Dosage for children should be determined by a pediatrician.
* **Older Adults:** Older adults should discuss usage with their physician, as they may be more susceptible to certain side effects.


**FAQs:**

* **Q: Can I take this supplement without consulting a doctor?** A: While generally considered safe, it's best to consult a doctor or registered dietitian, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions or are taking other medications.
* **Q: How should I store this supplement?** A: Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture, as per the instructions on the product label.
* **Q: Is it possible to overdose on this supplement?** A: Yes, excessive intake of certain components can lead to adverse effects.  Always follow the recommended dosage.
* **Q:  Can this supplement cure osteoporosis?** A: No, this is a nutritional supplement, not a treatment for osteoporosis. It supports bone health, but medical treatment is necessary for osteoporosis.
