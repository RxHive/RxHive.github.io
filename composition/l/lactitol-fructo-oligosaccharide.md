# Lactitol + Fructo Oligosaccharide

**Generic Name:** Lactitol + Fructo Oligosaccharide

**Usage:**

Lactitol and fructo-oligosaccharide (FOS) are often combined in over-the-counter products, primarily used as laxatives to relieve constipation.  The combination leverages the properties of both components. Lactitol, a sugar alcohol, draws water into the intestines, softening stools and promoting bowel movements. FOS, a type of prebiotic fiber, feeds beneficial bacteria in the gut, further promoting regular bowel habits and potentially improving gut health.  While the primary use is treating constipation, some formulations may also be used to help manage symptoms associated with diverticular disease (though this is not a primary treatment).  It's crucial to note that this combination is not intended for the treatment of underlying medical conditions causing constipation; it addresses the symptom itself.  Always consult a healthcare professional to determine the root cause of persistent constipation.


**Dosage:**

Dosage varies greatly depending on the specific product formulation (tablets, powder, syrup), age, and individual response.  There are no universally standardized dosages for lactitol + FOS combinations.  Always adhere to the instructions provided on the product label.  Typically, adults begin with a lower dose and gradually increase it as needed to achieve a comfortable bowel movement regularity.  Children's dosages are significantly lower and should only be administered under strict medical supervision.  It's vital to never exceed the recommended dosage without consulting a doctor or pharmacist.


**Side Effects:**

*   Gas
*   Bloating
*   Abdominal cramping
*   Diarrhea (especially with higher doses)
*   Flatulence
*   Nausea
*   Stomach discomfort

Less common, but potentially serious side effects include severe dehydration from excessive diarrhea. If experiencing any severe or persistent side effects, discontinue use and seek immediate medical attention.


**How it Works:**

Lactitol acts as a bulk-forming laxative. It's poorly absorbed in the small intestine, drawing water into the colon, thus softening the stool and stimulating bowel movements.  FOS, being a prebiotic, selectively promotes the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. This increased bacterial activity contributes to healthier bowel function and may improve overall gut health.  The synergistic effect of these two components results in a more effective and potentially gentler laxative action compared to using either substance alone.


**Popular Brand Names (India):**

Specific brand names containing this combination may vary widely across regions within India.  It's best to check with a local pharmacist or consult a physician for brand recommendations within your area.  Many products containing lactitol or FOS are available, but the specific combination is not always prominently labeled as "Lactitol + Fructo Oligosaccharide."


**Precautions:**

*   **Individuals with fructose malabsorption or intolerance:** Should avoid this combination, as lactitol and FOS contain sugars that may exacerbate symptoms.
*   **Diabetes:** May affect blood sugar levels. Individuals with diabetes should monitor their blood sugar closely while using this combination.
*   **Pregnancy and breastfeeding:**  Consult your doctor before using this product during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
*   **Interactions with other medications:** While generally well-tolerated, it's important to inform your doctor or pharmacist about all medications and supplements you're taking to avoid potential interactions.
*   **Children:**  Use only as directed by a physician.


**FAQs:**

*   **Q: How long does it take to work?** A: The effect varies from person to person, but generally, you might notice results within 24-72 hours.
*   **Q: Can I take this every day?** A:  This combination isn't intended for daily use. It should be used as needed to relieve constipation. Long-term use should be discussed with a doctor.
*   **Q: How should I store it?** A: Store according to the instructions on the product label, typically in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
*   **Q: What if I experience severe diarrhea?** A: Discontinue use immediately and contact your doctor.
