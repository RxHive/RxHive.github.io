# L-Ornithine L-Aspartate + Metadoxine + Vitamin B + Silymarin

**Generic Name:** L-Ornithine L-Aspartate + Metadoxine + Vitamin B + Silymarin

**Usage:** This combination of ingredients is often used to support liver health and function, particularly in cases of liver damage or disease.  The individual components work synergistically:

* **L-Ornithine L-Aspartate (LOLA):**  Plays a crucial role in the urea cycle, helping the liver eliminate ammonia, a toxic byproduct of protein metabolism. This is especially beneficial in conditions where ammonia levels are elevated, such as hepatic encephalopathy (a complication of liver disease). LOLA may also support liver regeneration.

* **Metadoxine (Methylenetetrahydrofolate):**  A form of folic acid, metadoxine acts as a methyl donor, potentially aiding in liver cell protection and function.  It's thought to improve liver cell membrane stability and reduce oxidative stress.

* **Vitamin B Complex:** Various B vitamins (the exact combination varies by product) are essential for numerous metabolic processes, including energy production and the proper function of the nervous system.  In the context of liver health, they support overall cellular function and repair.

* **Silymarin (Milk Thistle Extract):**  Contains silybin, a potent antioxidant that protects liver cells from damage caused by free radicals. It also exhibits anti-inflammatory properties and may help regenerate liver tissue.

This combination is primarily used to support the liver in cases of:

* **Alcoholic liver disease:**  Helping to reduce inflammation and improve liver function.
* **Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD):**  Potentially mitigating the progression of the disease.
* **Hepatic encephalopathy:** Reducing the accumulation of ammonia in the brain.
* **Other forms of liver damage:** Offering general liver support and protection.

Note that this combination is *supportive* therapy and should not replace other necessary treatments prescribed by a physician for underlying liver conditions.
