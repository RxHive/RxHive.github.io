# Levocetirizine + Paracetamol

**Generic Name: Levocetirizine + Paracetamol**

**Usage:**

Levocetirizine + Paracetamol is a combination medication used to relieve the symptoms of common cold, flu, and allergies.  It effectively tackles both the allergic symptoms and the associated fever and pain.

* **Primary Use:** This combination is primarily used to treat the symptoms of allergic rhinitis (hay fever), which include sneezing, runny nose, itchy eyes and nose, and congestion.  It also provides relief from the fever and body aches often accompanying these conditions.

* **Secondary Uses:**  It may be used to alleviate symptoms of other upper respiratory tract infections where fever and allergic-like symptoms are present. However, it’s crucial to understand this medication only treats *symptoms*, not the underlying cause of the infection.  It shouldn't replace medical advice for treating the actual illness.


**Dosage:**

Dosage varies depending on age and the specific product formulation (tablet, syrup, etc.).  Always follow the instructions on the label or as directed by your doctor or pharmacist.  **Never exceed the recommended dose.**

* **Adults:** Typically, the recommended dose is one tablet or the equivalent amount of syrup as directed, usually once or twice daily.

* **Children:** Dosage for children is strictly weight-based and determined by a healthcare professional.  Never administer adult formulations to children.  Syrups are commonly available for pediatric use, with dosage clearly marked based on weight or age.

* **Forms:**  This combination is available in tablet and syrup forms.  The choice depends on the patient’s age and preference.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common Side Effects:**  Drowsiness, headache, dry mouth, fatigue, nausea. These are usually mild and transient.

* **Less Common but Serious Side Effects:**  Although rare, more serious side effects like allergic reactions (rash, hives, difficulty breathing, swelling of the face, lips, tongue, or throat), dizziness, or stomach upset can occur.

* **Important Note:** If you experience any unusual or concerning side effects, stop taking the medication and consult a doctor or pharmacist immediately.
