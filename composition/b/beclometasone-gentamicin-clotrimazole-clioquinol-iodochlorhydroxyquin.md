# Beclometasone + Gentamicin + Clotrimazole + Clioquinol Iodochlorhydroxyquin

**Generic Name:** Beclometasone + Gentamicin + Clotrimazole + Clioquinol Iodochlorhydroxyquin

**Usage:** This combination medication is a topical preparation typically used to treat a variety of skin infections characterized by inflammation, bacterial infection, and fungal infection.  Its primary use is for managing conditions affecting the skin, such as:

* **Infections of the outer ear canal (Otitis externa):** This is a common use, particularly when the infection involves bacteria and fungi.
* **Infected eczema:**  This combination can help control both the infection and inflammation in eczema flares.
* **Other skin conditions:** It may sometimes be prescribed for other skin infections with similar characteristics, though this would be determined on a case-by-case basis by a doctor.

It's crucial to understand this is a combination drug targeting multiple aspects of infection. It should only be used under the direct supervision of a doctor, who can determine its suitability based on the specific infection and the individual's health.  It is not a general-purpose antibiotic or antifungal cream and shouldn't be used for self-treatment.

**Side Effects:** Common side effects can include:

* Burning or stinging sensation at the application site
* Skin irritation or redness
* Itching
* Dryness
* Thinning of the skin (with prolonged use)

Less common, but more serious side effects, although rare, may include:

* Allergic reactions (rash, hives, swelling)
* Skin discoloration
* Increased susceptibility to infection (if used for prolonged periods)

If you experience any unusual side effects, contact your healthcare provider immediately.


**How it Works:** This medication is a multi-component drug working through different mechanisms:

* **Beclometasone:** A corticosteroid that reduces inflammation and itching.
* **Gentamicin:** An aminoglycoside antibiotic effective against many bacteria commonly causing skin infections.
* **Clotrimazole:** An antifungal agent effective against various fungi that can cause skin infections.
* **Clioquinol (Iodochlorhydroxyquin):** An antimicrobial agent with both antibacterial and antifungal properties.


By combining these active ingredients, this medication tackles both the bacterial and fungal components of the infection, while also reducing inflammation and related symptoms.
