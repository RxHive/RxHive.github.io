# Bifidobacterium longum + Fructo Oligosaccharide + Lactobacillus Acidophilus + Streptococcus Thermophilus

**Generic Name:** Bifidobacterium longum + Fructo Oligosaccharide + Lactobacillus Acidophilus + Streptococcus Thermophilus

**Usage:** This combination of probiotics (Bifidobacterium longum, Lactobacillus acidophilus, Streptococcus thermophilus) and a prebiotic (fructo oligosaccharide, or FOS) is primarily used to support gut health.  It's often employed to alleviate symptoms associated with digestive issues.  While not a treatment for specific diseases, it aims to improve the balance of gut microbiota, which can positively impact various conditions.  Primary uses include the management of diarrhea (particularly traveler's diarrhea and antibiotic-associated diarrhea), irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) symptoms like bloating and abdominal discomfort, and the general improvement of digestive regularity. Secondary uses may involve supporting immune function, although more research is needed to solidify these claims.  It's crucial to understand this combination isn't a cure for underlying diseases but a supportive measure for improving gut health and relieving associated symptoms.

**Dosage:**  Dosage varies significantly depending on the specific product formulation (capsules, tablets, powders, yogurt drinks).  Always follow the manufacturer's instructions printed on the product label.  There are no universally established dosages for this specific combination, and recommendations may differ based on age and the individual's condition. Consulting a healthcare professional is recommended to determine the appropriate dosage and duration of use. Adult dosages generally range from one to several capsules or sachets daily, often taken with food. Pediatric dosages should always be determined by a doctor or pediatrician as it varies significantly by age and weight.  There are no injectable forms of this combination.


**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Gas, bloating, mild abdominal discomfort. These are often temporary and subside as the body adjusts.
* **Less Common, but Serious:**  Severe allergic reactions (rash, hives, difficulty breathing, swelling). While rare, these require immediate medical attention.
* **Note:** If any unusual or concerning side effects occur, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional immediately.

**How it Works:** This combination works synergistically.  The probiotics (Bifidobacterium longum, Lactobacillus acidophilus, and Streptococcus thermophilus) are beneficial bacteria that colonize the gut, helping to restore a healthy balance of gut flora.  FOS acts as a prebiotic, providing nourishment for these beneficial bacteria, promoting their growth and activity. By increasing the population of these beneficial bacteria, this combination helps improve digestion, reduce the growth of harmful bacteria, and alleviate symptoms of digestive discomfort.  This improvement in gut flora can also indirectly support immune function.

* **Drug Interactions:**  While generally well-tolerated, potential interactions with certain medications, particularly immunosuppressants, could occur.  Consult a doctor if you are taking other medications.
* **Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:**  While generally considered safe, it's best to consult a healthcare professional before using this product during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
* **Children and Older Adults:**  Dosage adjustments may be necessary for children and older adults. Always consult a healthcare professional to determine appropriate dosage.
