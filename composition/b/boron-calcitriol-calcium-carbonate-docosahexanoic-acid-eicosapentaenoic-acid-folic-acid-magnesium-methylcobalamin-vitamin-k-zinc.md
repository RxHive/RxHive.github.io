* **Folic Acid:** Crucial for cell growth and development, particularly important during pregnancy.
* **Methylcobalamin (Vitamin B12):**  Essential for nerve function, red blood cell production, and DNA synthesis.
* **Docosahexaenoic Acid (DHA) & Eicosapentaenoic Acid (EPA):** Omega-3 fatty acids known for their anti-inflammatory properties and benefits for cardiovascular health.
