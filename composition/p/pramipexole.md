# Pramipexole

**Generic Name: Pramipexole**

**Usage:**

Pramipexole is primarily used to treat Parkinson's disease.  It's a dopamine agonist, meaning it mimics the effects of dopamine, a neurotransmitter crucial for movement control. In Parkinson's, dopamine-producing brain cells are damaged, leading to tremors, rigidity, slow movement (bradykinesia), and postural instability.
