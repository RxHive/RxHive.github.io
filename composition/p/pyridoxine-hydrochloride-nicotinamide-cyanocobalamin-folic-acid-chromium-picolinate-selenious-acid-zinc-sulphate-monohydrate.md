# Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Nicotinamide + Cyanocobalamin + Folic Acid + Chromium Picolinate + Selenious acid + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

**Generic Name:** Pyridoxine Hydrochloride + Nicotinamide + Cyanocobalamin + Folic Acid + Chromium Picolinate + Selenious Acid + Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate

This combination product contains several essential vitamins and minerals often used to address nutritional deficiencies and support overall health.  It's crucial to understand that this isn't a cure-all, but rather a supplement to address specific deficiencies identified by a healthcare professional.


**Usage:**

The primary use of this multi-vitamin/mineral combination is to treat or prevent deficiencies in pyridoxine (vitamin B6), nicotinamide (vitamin B3), cyanocobalamin (vitamin B12), folic acid (vitamin B9), chromium, selenium, and zinc.  These deficiencies can manifest in various ways, depending on the specific nutrient lacking.  For example, B vitamin deficiencies can lead to anemia, fatigue, and neurological problems.  Zinc deficiency can impair immune function and wound healing. Selenium deficiency can increase the risk of certain health issues. Chromium plays a role in glucose metabolism.  This combination product aims to address these deficiencies simultaneously.

Secondary uses might include supporting overall well-being in individuals with poor diets or those with increased nutrient requirements (e.g., pregnant women, breastfeeding mothers, individuals recovering from illness). However, it's essential to remember that this product is not a substitute for a balanced diet.  A healthcare professional should always be consulted before using this or any supplement.


**Dosage:**

Dosage varies significantly depending on the specific formulation (tablet, capsule, liquid), the age and health status of the individual, and the severity of any deficiency.  There is no universally applicable dosage.  **It's absolutely critical to follow the instructions provided by your doctor or pharmacist and the label on the product.**  Self-medicating with this combination can be dangerous.  The dosage will be tailored to individual needs.

**Side Effects:**

* **Common:**  Generally, this combination is well-tolerated. However, mild gastrointestinal upset (nausea, diarrhea, stomach cramps) may occur.
* **Less Common but Serious:** Allergic reactions (rash, itching, swelling, difficulty breathing) are possible, though rare.  High doses of certain components can have adverse effects.  For example, excessive zinc can cause nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and copper deficiency.  Excessive selenium can lead to hair loss, nail changes, and gastrointestinal problems.  If any serious side effect develops, seek immediate medical attention.


**How it Works:**

Each component in this combination works individually to fulfill its specific role in the body.  For example, vitamin B12 is crucial for red blood cell formation and nerve function; folic acid is essential for cell growth and development; zinc is vital for immune function and wound healing; selenium acts as an antioxidant; and chromium assists in glucose metabolism.  The combination provides a synergistic effect, addressing multiple nutritional needs simultaneously.


**Precautions:**

* **Contraindications:**  Individuals with known allergies to any of the ingredients should avoid this product.
* **Drug Interactions:**  This combination may interact with certain medications.  For instance, some medications can affect the absorption or metabolism of these nutrients.  It's imperative to inform your doctor or pharmacist about all medications, supplements, and herbal remedies you are taking.
* **Specific Populations:** Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult their doctor before taking this supplement.  Children’s dosages need to be determined by a healthcare professional based on age and weight.  Individuals with pre-existing medical conditions should also seek professional advice before use.
* **Storage:** Store according to the product label instructions, typically in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
